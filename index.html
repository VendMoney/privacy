1. Introduction

Vend Pty Ltd (“Vend”, “we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Vend mobile application and related services (the “App” or “Services”). Vend is a digital payments and autonomous commerce platform that may enable users to interact with tokenized loyalty systems, digital wallets, on-chain infrastructure, and participating merchants.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable global privacy laws, including the GDPR (EU/UK), the Australian Privacy Act 1988, and the CCPA/CPRA (California), among others.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only information that is necessary to operate and improve the Services.

A. Information you provide

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth (if required for compliance)
  • Government ID (for KYC/AML compliance where applicable)
  • Payment or wallet details
  • Communications with us

B. Automatically collected information

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • App version
  • Usage data (feature interactions)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Approximate location (if enabled)

Vend does not collect precise GPS location unless you explicitly enable it and your device permissions allow it.

C. Blockchain & transaction data

Because Vend may integrate blockchain infrastructure, some information may be recorded on public blockchains, including public wallet addresses, transaction hashes, and smart contract interactions. Blockchain data is inherently public and may be permanent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use data to:

  • Provide and operate the App and Services
  • Process payments and digital asset transactions
  • Verify identity (where legally required)
  • Prevent fraud and financial crime
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve product performance and reliability
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send service-related notifications

We do not sell personal data.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the EU/UK, we process personal data under one or more of these legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity (to provide Services you request)
  • Legal obligation (e.g., compliance, KYC/AML where applicable)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., fraud prevention, security, improving Services)
  • Consent (where required; you may withdraw at any time)

5. Sharing of Information

A. Service providers

We may share data with vendors who help us operate the Services, such as:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Identity verification providers
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers
  • Blockchain infrastructure partners

Service providers are contractually required to protect your information.

B. Legal & regulatory authorities

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, subpoena, or to comply with applicable regulations (including AML/KYC where applicable).

C. Business transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. Where required, we implement safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), adequacy decisions, or equivalent legal mechanisms.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal and regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Blockchain transaction data may not be erasable due to decentralized network design.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, object, and request portability of your data, as well as withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@vendmoney.com. We may verify your identity before processing requests.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents may have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, correct inaccurate information, limit certain uses of sensitive personal information, and opt out of sale/sharing.

Vend does not sell personal information.

10. Children’s Privacy

Vend is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

11. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, which may include encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), encrypted storage where appropriate, access controls, secure cloud infrastructure, and multi-factor authentication for sensitive systems. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

12. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate third-party services (e.g., blockchain networks, wallet providers, payment processors, analytics providers). These parties have their own privacy policies, and their practices are not controlled by Vend.

13. App Store & Google Play Compliance

Vend aims to disclose applicable data collection and usage in the Apple App Store privacy labels and Google Play Data Safety section. Vend does not track users across third-party apps for advertising without consent.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice, email (where appropriate), and/or an updated policy posted to this page.
